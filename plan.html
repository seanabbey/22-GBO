CP1406 - 2016 Group Project Plan

Group Number 22

Members: Matthew Chilcott, Nando Retallick-Boundey, Sean Abbey, Bonnie Thompson.

Goals

The Great Barrier Reef Orchestra aims to attract more members, sponsors and donors. They also want to increase their target audeince range to one similar to that of members in their Orchestra meaning ages roughly 13-73 years old.

Success Evaluation

In order to evaluate their success they can monitor the ticket sales prior to the websites launch, and ticket sales after. Alongside this they could possibly ask the ticket sales venues to record the age of people making purchases so that they could then compare this data to their ideal audience ages.
They can also keep track of the rate of how many people were signing up (say monthly) to be donors, sponsors and members prior to the websites launch and compare that to the figures after the websites launch..

Target Audience

  1. They wish to appeal to an audience similar to that of the members in their Orchestra, so the target audience would range from 14 to 73 years old.
  2. To appeal to our target audience we will incorporate colours and reasonable sized fonts that appeal to both younger and older generations. The site will be proffesional and easy to navigate in order to ensure the non tech savvy are still able to use it. The site will also be very adaptable in order to appeal to modern tech crowds who access the web from various devices, not just computers.
